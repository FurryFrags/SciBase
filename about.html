About SciBase 🚀

Open-source science for everyone, everywhere.

SciBase is a massive, enthusiastic open-source home for science simulations made in HTML. Our mission is simple but huge: make interactive science learning available to anyone with a browser, regardless of budget, country, or classroom setup.

We believe curiosity should be free. From orbital mechanics to ecosystems and chemistry labs, every simulation here is meant to help people explore, experiment, and understand the world more deeply.

Our mission

  • Help students learn through interaction, not memorization.
  • Help teachers access useful demos without expensive software.
  • Help self-learners, makers, and researchers prototype ideas quickly.
  • Grow a joyful, community-driven library of simulations for everyone.

Fun fact 🤖

The entire site is AI-generated. From the structure and styling to the simulation concepts and supporting copy, AI tools helped shape SciBase from the ground up.

That means the project can evolve quickly: ideas can be prototyped in minutes, content can be expanded rapidly, and contributors can focus on improving quality, accuracy, and creativity together. We see AI as a collaborative co-builder that helps us ship more science experiences to more people, faster.

This project is proudly community-powered and open-source. If it helps you, we hope you'll help someone else discover it too. 🌍💙
